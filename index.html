Who We Are

Welcome to Our Church

We reveal the Christ in men to men.

At DivineWorld Global Outreach, we are a community of believers committed to understanding and living out the fullness of Christ's finished work. We believe in the transformative power of God's grace and the truth that sets us free.

Our Mission

Called to Proclaim

DivineWorld Global Outreach Ministries is called to proclaim the finished works of Christ on the cross, furnishing believers with the truth of Grace, raising a people empowered with the Word, made fit to live victorious, enforcing God's dominion to the World.
